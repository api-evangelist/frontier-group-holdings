BOULDER, Colo. — Boulderthon , presented by PUMA, has announced the launch of its brand new campaign, Race Together, Fly Together Sweepstakes, presented in partnership with Frontier Airlines , America’s Greenest Airline and Denver’s hometown carrier. The campaign welcomes run clubs nationwide to apply, with the grand prize winner receiving up to $2,000 in Frontier flight credits, five complimentary race entries and a two-night hotel stay in Boulder.
