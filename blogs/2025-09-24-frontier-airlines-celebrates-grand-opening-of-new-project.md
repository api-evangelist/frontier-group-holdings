SAN JUAN, Puerto Rico – September 24, 2025 – Frontier Airlines ( Nasdaq: ULCC ) celebrated the grand opening of its new Project Management Office in Guaynabo today, marking the airline’s latest investment on the island and deepening of its partnership with Puerto Rico. The facility will house employees who provide project oversight for a variety of major company initiatives. Currently, eight employees work at the facility, with further growth expected.
