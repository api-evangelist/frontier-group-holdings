DENVER – April 22, 2026 – Frontier Airlines ( Nasdaq: ULCC ) today launched its 2026 GoWild Summer Pass at its lowest ever introductory price. Consumers can purchase the pass now for just $199 and unlock immediate access, rather than waiting for the typical pass period to begin, enjoying more than five months of unlimited flights across Frontier’s expansive network. In addition, as part of a limited time launch promotion, GoWild pass holders will have even more flexibility as they plan ahead for their summer adventures.
