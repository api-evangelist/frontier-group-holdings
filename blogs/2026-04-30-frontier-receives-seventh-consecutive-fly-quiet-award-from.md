SEATTLE – April 30, 2026 – Frontier was recognized by the Port of Seattle this week for minimizing the noise impacts of its operations at the Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA), receiving its seventh consecutive Fly Quiet Award. The Port’s Sustainable Century and Fly Quiet Awards acknowledge aviation and maritime companies and organizations for their sustainability and equity efforts which positively impact the greater Seattle community. Frontier received high scores for its consistently lower noise levels compared to other domestic carriers.
