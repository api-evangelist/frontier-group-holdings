DENVER – September 4, 2025 – Frontier Airlines ( Nasdaq: ULCC ) announced today it will launch 22 new routes later this year, increasing service across the United States, the Caribbean and Latin America. The new routes, which will launch in November and December, include new service from Atlanta, Dallas-Fort Worth, Chicago and more in the U.S., and new international routes to destinations in Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico and more.
