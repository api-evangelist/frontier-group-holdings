DENVER - December 11, 2025 - Frontier is spreading some special kid-focused holiday cheer this season with limited-edition holiday trading cards featuring four of the airline’s famous plane tail animals. Kids flying Frontier through the end of January can request one of the cards, which highlight Blanco the Polar Bear, Flo the Flamingo, Ruby the Reindeer or Verde the Resplendent Quetzal, from a crew member during their flight. The back of each card contains information on the featured animal species, along with details on which Frontier aircraft has the animal on its tail.
