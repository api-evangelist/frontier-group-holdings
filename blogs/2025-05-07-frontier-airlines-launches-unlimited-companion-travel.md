DENVER – May 7, 2025 – Your summer vacation deserves a plus one! Today, ultra-low fare carrier Frontier Airlines ( Nasdaq: ULCC ) launched its free*, unlimited companion travel benefit for FRONTIER Miles SM Elite Platinum and Diamond Status members. The airline is setting a new standard for companion travel with unparalleled flexibility, giving qualifying members the freedom to choose any friend or family member they wish to accompany them for free* on any Frontier flight.
