DENVER – August 20, 2025 – Frontier Airlines ( Nasdaq: ULCC ) wants to be your new low fare airline, and is celebrating the end of summer with a limited-time sale to destinations throughout the U.S., the Caribbean, and Latin America. Today and tomorrow, travelers can access fares as low as $38* roundtrip on a wide variety of routes. To take advantage of this limited time offer, consumers must book travel on FlyFrontier.com or the Frontier mobile app by 11:59 pm EDT on August 21.
