DENVER – August 25, 2025 – Frontier Airlines ( Nasdaq: ULCC ), America’s Low Fare Airline, today announced an unprecedented lineup of loyalty offers. For a limited time, FRONTIER Airlines World Mastercard ® cardmembers can earn companion certificates starting at just $3,000 in everyday spend. New FRONTIER Miles members can take advantage of Miles Match from other airlines, allowing them to match up to their combined mileage balances across multiple qualifying airlines, and travelers with frequent flier memberships at select carriers can unlock instant Elite Gold status at Frontier.
