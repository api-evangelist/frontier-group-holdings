DENVER – November 20, 2025 – Frontier Airlines ( Nasdaq: ULCC ) will launch four new routes across seven airports early next year, increasing the affordable travel options offered in New Jersey, Florida, Illinois, Utah and Arizona. On Jan. 21, 2026, thrice-weekly nonstop service between Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) and Orlando International Airport will begin.
